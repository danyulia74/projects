About cakeshop online

CakeShop online store is a supermarket for professional confectioners and housewives. We are engaged in retail and small wholesale trade. In our shop you can find a large selection of tools for confectioners, confectionery raw materials and sugar mastic, food colors and flavors, pastry bags and cream nozzles, racks for cakes and cupcakes, packaging for any pastry, silicone molds and ice mats, pads and stands for cakes, bakeware and cake assemblies, cuts for gingerbread and plastics for mastic, decor of confectionery and accessories for decorating festive tables, as well as a lot of interesting and useful things.
